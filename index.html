Turmeric Fingers – Premium Grade

At ESHOVI INTERNATIONAL, we supply high-quality turmeric fingers sourced from select farms in India. Our turmeric is carefully harvested, sun-dried, and sorted to deliver exceptional purity, vibrant golden color, and a rich natural aroma.

  • Origin: India (Maharashtra)
  • Type: Whole Dried Turmeric Fingers
  • Curcumin Content: 3% to 5% (Natural)
  • Moisture: Below 12%
  • Processing: Sun-dried & machine cleaned
  • Usage: Spices, medicinal, cosmetics, food processing, and dye industries
  • Packaging: As per buyer requirement (5kg, 10kg, 25kg, or bulk)

We ensure strict quality checks at every stage and offer competitive pricing with global export compliance.

Turmeric Powder – Premium Quality

ESHOVI INTERNATIONAL offers finely ground turmeric powder made from handpicked turmeric fingers, ensuring maximum purity, natural color, and high curcumin content. Our turmeric powder is free from additives and preservatives— perfect for culinary, medicinal, and industrial applications.

  • Origin: India (Maharashtra)
  • Type: Pure Ground Turmeric Powder
  • Curcumin Content: 3% to 5% (Natural)
  • Color: Bright yellow to deep orange
  • Aroma: Strong, earthy, and authentic
  • Usage: Spices, health supplements, cosmetics, food & beverage industry
  • Packaging: Customized as per requirement (100g, 500g, 1kg, 25kg, bulk)

We follow strict quality control measures to maintain consistency, hygiene, and international export standards.

Pure. Potent. Trusted Worldwide.

  • Packaging: Customized as per requirement (10g, 50g, 100g, bulk)
